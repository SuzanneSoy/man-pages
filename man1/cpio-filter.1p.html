NAME

cpio-filter - transform a cpio archive  

SYNOPSIS

    cpio-filter [--exclude <PATTERN>] [--in-place] [<cpio file>]
 

DESCRIPTION

Transform a cpio archive on the fly. Reads on stdin and output on stderr
